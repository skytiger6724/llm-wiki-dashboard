### 第一階段：AI 味診斷分析


- **罐頭式開場**：無論是「非常認同」還是「Spot on」，都是 AI 最愛的開場白，這會讓讀者自動開啟「略過模式」。

- **名詞堆砌（Noun Dumping）**：原文充斥著抽象名詞，例如「跨領域的整合直覺」、「複雜情境下的道德決策」。這不是人類說話的方式。

- **過度完美的結構**：句子長短太過一致，缺乏情緒起伏。

John hits the nail on the head here.


Let’s be blunt: AI has officially made technical execution a commodity. "Knowing how to do it" is no longer the trophy—it's just the entry ticket. The real battlefield has shifted back to the stuff a machine simply can’t faked.


While everyone is racing to be "faster" with AI, the real premium is moving toward the "human messiness" that algorithms hate: that gut feeling in a moral gray area, the empathy needed to lead a burnt-out team, and the interdisciplinary vision that connects dots no one else sees. These aren't just "soft skills"; they are "durable assets."


The top 1% in the AI era won't be the best prompt engineers. They’ll be the ones who provide the soul, the ethics, and the human connection that gives tech a reason to exist. After all, a fast car is useless if the driver doesn't know where they're going.

### 第三階段：編輯筆記（關鍵調整）



- **打破禮貌的假象**：把「非常認同」改為「說得太透徹了」或「Let’s be blunt」。這讓評論看起來像是一個真實的人在參與對話，而不是機器在拍馬屁。

- **具象化「技術門檻」**：用「門票」或「Entry ticket」來形容技術，暗示它已經不再是高不可攀的優勢，而只是基礎。

- **強化對比**：使用了「代碼再美，沒有方向也是白搭」這類更具攻擊性（與記憶點）的對比句式。

- **去術語化**：將「跨領域整合直覺」簡化為「把碎片資訊串成遠見」或「Connects dots no one else sees」。
