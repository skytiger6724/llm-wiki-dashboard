**English Version:**
Meta's aggressive pivot toward "AI-driven efficiency" represents a high-stakes gamble that risks devaluing the very human intuition that built the social giant. While AI can undoubtedly streamline routine tasks and code generation, it remains a sophisticated tool rather than a holistic replacement for the "institutional knowledge" and collaborative synergy found in seasoned teams. By gutting the workforce to fund GPU clusters, Meta may find itself with immense computing power but a hollowed-out creative core. Over-reliance on AI-assisted solo output often sacrifices the nuanced depth and innovative spark that only a diverse, psychologically secure human workforce can provide.
