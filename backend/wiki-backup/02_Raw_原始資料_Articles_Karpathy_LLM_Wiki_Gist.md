# Andrej Karpathy: [[LLM Wiki]] Philosophy

Source: https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f

## Content Summary
Andrej Karpathy advocates for a "[[LLM Wiki]]" approach to knowledge management. Instead of simple RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) which performs search over raw documents, the [[LLM Wiki]] approach involves using LLMs to "compile" raw documents into a structured, interlinked wiki.

Key points:
- Raw sources are immutable truth.
- The Wiki is a dynamic, compiled layer.
- LLMs act as the "librarians" or "editors" that update the Wiki as new sources are ingested.
- This creates a "compounding artifact" of knowledge.
