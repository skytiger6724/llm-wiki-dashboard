本指南提取自 `Science_Prompt` 资料，整理了学术论文写作全流程的 10 大核心 Prompt（包含中英文双语指令）。涵盖了从开题、章节写作、文献整合到修改润色的四大阶段，帮助研究者系统性地提高写作效率与质量。
## 一、 开题与框架搭建类
### 1. 研究主题拆解与框架设计
- **中文指令**：基于主题《[具体主题]》，结合该领域近 3 年顶刊研究热点，拆解核心与次要研究问题，搭建完整论文框架，各章节标注核心写作要点与逻辑脉络，从理论、方法、内容维度分析创新点，从数据、设计、分析维度梳理研究难点。
- **英文指令**：Based on the topic 《[Specific topic]》, combined with the research hotspots of top journals in this field in the past 3 years, decompose core and secondary research questions, build a complete thesis framework, mark the core writing points and logical context for each chapter, analyze innovation points from the dimensions of theory, method and content, and sort out research difficulties from the dimensions of data, design and analysis.
### 2. 文献综述框架与热点梳理
- **中文指令**：我将提供 [X] 篇《[主题]》的中英文文献摘要或全文，用主题聚类法梳理文献脉络，区分经典理论、研究争议、研究空白，搭建文献综述章节框架，标注各部分核心引用文献，从视角、方法、对象维度总结研究趋势。
- **英文指令**：I will provide [X] Chinese and English literature abstracts or full texts on 《[Topic]》, sort out the literature context by thematic clustering method, distinguish classic theories, research controversies and research gaps, build a literature review chapter framework, mark the core cited literatures for each part, and summarize research trends from the dimensions of perspective, method and object.
## 二、 章节写作与深化类
### 3. 研究方法细化与可行性论证
- **中文指令**：基于研究问题《[具体问题]》，设计详细研究方案，含研究设计、数据来源、样本量计算、变量操作化定义、数据分析方法，从时间、成本、伦理维度论证可行性，输出研究方法章节初稿。
- **英文指令**：Based on the research question 《[Specific question]》, design a detailed research plan including research design, data source, sample size calculation, variable operationalization definition and data analysis method, demonstrate the feasibility from the dimensions of time, cost and ethics, and output the first draft of the research method chapter.
### 4. 数据分析与结果呈现
- **中文指令**：我将提供 [数据描述]，请进行 [具体分析方法]，生成符合 [格式标准] 的高清图表，按描述性结果→验证性结果→异常结果的结构撰写结果部分，客观呈现数据特征、结合假设解释结果、分析异常数据价值，语言符合学术规范。
- **英文指令**：I will provide [Data description], please conduct [Specific analysis method], generate high-definition charts in line with [Format standard], and write the results section according to the structure of descriptive results -> confirmatory results -> abnormal results. Objectively present data characteristics, explain results in combination with hypotheses, and analyze the value of abnormal data with academic normative language.
### 5. 讨论章节深度展开
- **中文指令**：基于我的研究结果 [简要概括]，撰写讨论章节，按结果与文献对比→不一致原因分析→理论贡献→实践启示→研究局限性→未来研究方向展开，对比文献异同、分析差异原因、阐述理论与实践价值、客观说明局限性、提出针对性研究方向，逻辑呼应研究问题。
- **英文指令**：Based on my research results [Brief summary], write the discussion chapter according to the structure of comparison of results with literature -> analysis of reasons for inconsistency -> theoretical contributions -> practical implications -> research limitations -> future research directions. Compare the similarities and differences of literatures, analyze the causes of differences, elaborate theoretical and practical values, objectively explain limitations, and put forward targeted research directions, with logic responding to research questions.
## 三、 文献整合与引用类
### 6. 文献引用优化与格式统一
- **中文指令**：检查以下段落 [粘贴内容] 的文献引用，按 [格式标准] 统一标注为 [标注形式]，修正引用错误、补充必要引用，确保引用与参考文献一一对应，准确简洁且符合学术规范，保留原论证逻辑。
- **英文指令**：Check the literature citations in the following paragraph [Paste content], unify the annotation into [Annotation form] according to [Format standard], correct citation errors, supplement necessary citations, ensure the one-to-one correspondence between citations and references, be accurate and concise in line with academic norms, and retain the original argumentation logic.
### 7. 间接引用改写（降重+保留原意）
- **中文指令**：针对以下文献观点 [粘贴原文+引用文献] 进行间接引用改写，保留核心观点与逻辑，实现降重且语句通顺，按 [格式标准] 标注文内引用，适配论文上下文语境，确保衔接自然。
- **英文指令**：Rewrite the following literature viewpoints [Paste original text + cited literature] into indirect citations, retain the core viewpoints and logic, achieve plagiarism reduction with smooth sentences, mark in-text citations according to [Format standard], adapt to the thesis context, and ensure natural connection.
### 8. 参考文献列表格式整理
- **中文指令**：整理以下参考文献 [粘贴列表]，按 [格式标准] 统一排版，覆盖各类文献类型，修正信息错误、补充缺失的 DOI/ISBN/ISSN，区分中英文文献格式，按作者姓氏首字母升序排序，标注文献序号并与文内引用对应，以表格形式输出。
- **英文指令**：Sort out the following references [Paste list], typeset uniformly according to [Format standard], cover all types of literatures, correct information errors, supplement missing DOI/ISBN/ISSN, distinguish Chinese and English literature formats, sort in ascending order by the first letter of the author's surname, mark literature serial numbers corresponding to in-text citations, and output in table form.
## 四、 修改润色与合规类
### 9. 学术表达润色（语法+严谨性）
- **中文指令**：润色以下论文段落 [粘贴内容]，适配 [目标场景] 语言风格，修正语法与标点错误，替换口语化模糊化词汇，强化逻辑连接，使用第三人称表述，保留原论证逻辑与核心观点，提升论证严谨性。
- **英文指令**：Polish the following thesis paragraph [Paste content] to adapt to the language style of [Target scenario], correct grammatical and punctuation errors, replace colloquial and vague words, strengthen logical connections, use the third person, retain the original argumentation logic and core viewpoints, and improve the argumentation rigor.
### 10. 降重改写（保留逻辑+学术性）
- **中文指令**：针对以下段落 [粘贴内容] 按 [查重平台] 标准降重改写，不改变核心观点、逻辑与结构，对 [指定部分] 精准降重，通过句式调整、词汇替换、结构重构实现降重，保持学术严谨性与论文整体风格，标注修改位置。
- **英文指令**：Rewrite the following paragraph [Paste content] for plagiarism reduction according to [Duplicate checking platform] standards, without changing the core viewpoints, logic and structure, accurately reduce the weight of [Designated part] through sentence adjustment, vocabulary replacement and structure reconstruction, maintain academic rigor and the overall thesis style, and mark the modified positions.
