# 實體：Cisco 360 Partner Program

## 核心定義
Cisco 合作夥伴評等系統，透過 Partner Value Index (PVI) 評分決定合作夥伴等級 (Partner / Preferred)。由四維度加權計算：Foundational (25%) + Capabilities (20-50%) + Performance (20-40%) + Engagement (10-30%)。

## 關鍵事實
- **評分門檻**: Partner (PVI ≥ 5.0)、Preferred (PVI ≥ 7.5)
- **[[Dynasafe]] 現況 (2026/2)**: Networking (8.50 ✅ Preferred)、Security (7.50 ✅ Preferred)、Cloud & AI (6.35 ⚠️ 待衝刺)、Service (5.03)、Splunk (3.25)、Collaboration (4.80)
- **提分關鍵 - Foundational**: MSPM 成熟度從 Basic 提升至 Intermediate/Expert
- **提分關鍵 - Capabilities**: Black Belt 認證 (Stage 1-3)、Career Certs (Associate 1分 → Expert 10分)
- **提分關鍵 - Performance**: YoY ACV/TCV 增長、EA/WPA 購買滲透率、AI Bookings
- **提分關鍵 - Engagement**: LCI 流程 (Onboard + Adopt 申報)、Splunk 客戶成功案例
- **公式**: PVI = Σ (Metric Index × Metric Weight × Category Weight)
