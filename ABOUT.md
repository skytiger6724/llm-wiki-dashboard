# About LLM Wiki Dashboard

## 🛸 The Vision
LLM Wiki Dashboard isn't just a visualization tool; it's the **frontend for your Second Brain**. Inspired by Andrej Karpathy's philosophy, we believe that AI should not just "retrieve" knowledge but "compile" it into a structured, evolving, and interconnected wiki.

## 🧠 Architectural Philosophy: Stellar Map & Local RAG

### 1. Stellar Map Aesthetic (v3.0+)
We moved away from traditional force-directed graphs to a **Galactic Visualization** model. Knowledge nodes are treated as stars within a constellation.
- **Glowing Node Rendering**: Multi-layered radial gradients simulate stellar intensity.
- **Dynamic Energy Flows**: Particles represent semantic relationships and knowledge transmission.

### 2. Local RAG Semantic Engine (v3.8+)
Security and privacy are non-negotiable. Our backend integrates a **100% Local RAG pipeline**:
- **Ollama**: Local inference for embeddings (`nomic-embed-text`).
- **ChromaDB**: Native persistent vector storage.
- **Semantic Search**: Breaking the "keyword barrier" by enabling conceptual discovery across 4,000+ nodes.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React 19, Vite, Force-Graph-2D, Lucide Icons.
- **Backend**: Express.js, Node-Python Bridge.
- **AI Core**: Python 3.14, Ollama, ChromaDB.
